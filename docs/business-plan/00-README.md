# Doxa Enterprise — Business Plan

**Doxa — The Trust Layer for Enterprise AI.**
*Atlan gives AI context. Doxa gives AI context you can put in front of an auditor.*

This folder is the complete, investor-grade business plan for Doxa Enterprise: a compliance-grade data &
AI governance platform for security-conscious enterprises and government.

> ⚠️ **Stage disclosure (read first).** Doxa is **pre-seed and pre-product**. Today we have compliance-grade
> infrastructure (a .NET Aspire / Azure scaffold) and detailed architecture & compliance specifications —
> **not** shipped product or revenue. This plan describes what we will build with the raise. Every financial
> figure and every person/customer named is **illustrative or a representative placeholder** unless explicitly
> stated otherwise. See the assumptions log in [10-appendix.md](10-appendix.md).
